About Us

We are passionate fishing enthusiasts dedicated to promoting sustainable fishing practices in Rwanda. Our team consists of experienced local anglers who know the best spots and techniques for successful fishing in the region. Founded in 2020, we've helped thousands of beginners start their fishing journey and supported experienced anglers in improving their skills.

Buying Tips

  • Check for clear, bright eyes and red gills
  • Press the flesh - should be firm and spring back
  • Best prices early morning
  • Negotiate prices for bulk purchases
